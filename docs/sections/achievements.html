Section 12: Achievements, Challenges and Aspirations

A straightforward text-only section where the doctor records their achievements, aspirations, and items for discussion with the appraiser. There are no repeatable items or conditional fields — just four textarea fields capturing reflective narrative.

Section name: Section_12_AchievementsChallanges (note: the typo "Challanges" is in the actual XFA data)
Fluent accessor: XfaPdf::achievements()
Navigation label: Achievements, challenges and aspirations

What's in this section?

FieldTypeWhat it is
achievementsTextareaNotable achievements or challenges since last appraisal
aspirationsTextareaCareer aspirations and plans for the coming year
forDiscussionTextareaAnything additional to discuss with the appraiser
appraiserCommentsTextareaAppraiser comments (mirrors to Section 19)

This section has no repeatable items and no conditional fields. All four fields are standalone multiline text areas.

Setup

Before filling any fields, load the PDF in your controller:

use Xfa\Pdf\Facades\XfaPdf;

// Load the PDF file
XfaPdf::setFile(storage_path('app/xfa-pdf/appraisal.pdf'));

Reading existing data

Read all fields in this section at once:

// Read everything in Achievements, Challenges and Aspirations
$data = XfaPdf::read('Section_12_AchievementsChallanges');

// Or using the fluent accessor
$data = XfaPdf::achievements()->read();

// $data looks like:
// [
//     'achievements' => 'Completed Advanced Certificate in Diabetes Management...',
//     'aspirations' => 'Planning to develop a community respiratory clinic...',
//     'forDiscussion' => 'Would like to discuss options for sabbatical leave...',
//     'appraiserComments' => 'Dr Smith has demonstrated strong clinical leadership...',
// ]

Read a single field:

$achievements = XfaPdf::achievements()->readField('achievements');
// 'Completed Advanced Certificate in Diabetes Management...'

$aspirations = XfaPdf::achievements()->readField('aspirations');
// 'Planning to develop a community respiratory clinic...'

Text Fields

All four fields in this section are multiline text areas intended for reflective narrative. Each can hold substantial text describing the doctor's professional journey over the appraisal period.

Achievements and challenges

Field: achievements

The doctor's notable achievements or challenges since their last appraisal. This is typically a reflective summary covering clinical work, leadership activities, educational accomplishments, and any significant obstacles encountered during the appraisal year.

XfaPdf::achievements()->updateField(
    'achievements',
    'Key achievements this year include:\n'
    . '- Completed Advanced Certificate in Diabetes Management (University of Leicester)\n'
    . '- Led the implementation of a new chronic disease review pathway across the practice, '
    . 'reducing unplanned hospital admissions by 15%\n'
    . '- Successfully supervised two GP registrars through their clinical placements\n'
    . '- Published a case report in the British Journal of General Practice\n\n'
    . 'Challenges:\n'
    . '- Managing increased patient demand following the closure of a neighbouring practice\n'
    . '- Balancing clinical workload with expanding PCN leadership responsibilities'
);

Aspirations

Field: aspirations

The doctor's career aspirations and plans for the coming year. This covers professional development goals, changes in clinical focus, new roles under consideration, and longer-term career direction.

XfaPdf::achievements()->updateField(
    'aspirations',
    'In the coming year I plan to:\n'
    . '- Develop a community-based respiratory clinic within the PCN, '
    . 'offering spirometry and COPD management closer to patients\n'
    . '- Complete the Diploma in Medical Education to formalise my teaching role\n'
    . '- Explore opportunities for involvement in clinical research through '
    . 'the NIHR Clinical Research Network\n'
    . '- Continue to build leadership skills through the NHS Leadership Academy programme\n\n'
    . 'Longer term, I am considering a portfolio career combining clinical practice '
    . 'with medical education and health service management.'
);

Items for discussion

Field: forDiscussion

Anything additional the doctor wishes to discuss with the appraiser during the appraisal meeting. This might include concerns about workload, requests for support, ideas for service development, or personal matters affecting their practice.

XfaPdf::achievements()->updateField(
    'forDiscussion',
    'I would like to discuss the following during our meeting:\n'
    . '1. Options for a short sabbatical (3 months) to complete the Diploma '
    . 'in Medical Education - timing and locum cover arrangements\n'
    . '2. The impact of the neighbouring practice closure on our workload '
    . 'and whether additional clinical sessions are sustainable\n'
    . '3. Mentoring opportunities - I would value being paired with a senior '
    . 'GP who has experience in health service management'
);

Appraiser comments

Field: appraiserComments

Written by the appraiser, summarising their view of the doctor's achievements and aspirations. This field mirrors content to Section 19 (Appraisal Summary), so anything written here may appear in the final summary output.

XfaPdf::achievements()->updateField(
    'appraiserComments',
    'Dr Smith has had an impressive year with clear achievements in both '
    . 'clinical practice and education. The reduction in unplanned admissions '
    . 'through the new chronic disease pathway is particularly noteworthy and '
    . 'demonstrates strong clinical leadership.\n\n'
    . 'The aspirations for the coming year are well-considered and realistic. '
    . 'I support the plan to pursue the Diploma in Medical Education and will '
    . 'help explore sabbatical options with the practice. The interest in '
    . 'clinical research is commendable and aligns well with the PCN strategy.\n\n'
    . 'We discussed the workload pressures from the neighbouring practice '
    . 'closure and agreed to revisit this at the mid-year review.'
);

Complete Controller Example

Here's a full example filling the entire Achievements section in a Laravel controller:

use Xfa\Pdf\Facades\XfaPdf;

class AppraisalController extends Controller
{
    public function fillAchievements(Request $request)
    {
        $pdfPath = storage_path('app/xfa-pdf/appraisal.pdf');
        XfaPdf::setFile($pdfPath);

        // -----------------------------------------------
        // Update all four fields at once
        // -----------------------------------------------
        XfaPdf::achievements()->update([
            'achievements' =>
                'Key achievements this year include:\n'
                . '- Completed Advanced Certificate in Diabetes Management\n'
                . '- Led implementation of chronic disease review pathway, '
                . 'reducing unplanned admissions by 15%\n'
                . '- Supervised two GP registrars through clinical placements\n'
                . '- Published case report in BJGP\n\n'
                . 'Challenges:\n'
                . '- Managing increased demand after neighbouring practice closure\n'
                . '- Balancing clinical workload with PCN leadership duties',

            'aspirations' =>
                'Plans for the coming year:\n'
                . '- Develop community respiratory clinic within the PCN\n'
                . '- Complete Diploma in Medical Education\n'
                . '- Explore NIHR Clinical Research Network involvement\n'
                . '- Continue NHS Leadership Academy programme',

            'forDiscussion' =>
                '1. Sabbatical options for Diploma in Medical Education\n'
                . '2. Workload impact from neighbouring practice closure\n'
                . '3. Mentoring opportunities in health service management',

            'appraiserComments' =>
                'Dr Smith has had an impressive year demonstrating strong '
                . 'clinical leadership. The chronic disease pathway work is '
                . 'particularly noteworthy. Aspirations are well-considered '
                . 'and realistic. We will revisit workload pressures at the '
                . 'mid-year review.',
        ]);

        // -----------------------------------------------
        // Save the PDF
        // -----------------------------------------------
        XfaPdf::save();

        return redirect()->back()->with(
            'success',
            'Achievements section saved.'
        );
    }

    /**
     * Read and display existing Achievements data.
     */
    public function showAchievements()
    {
        $pdfPath = storage_path('app/xfa-pdf/appraisal.pdf');
        XfaPdf::setFile($pdfPath);

        $data = XfaPdf::achievements()->read();

        return view('appraisal.achievements', [
            'achievements'      => $data['achievements'] ?? '',
            'aspirations'       => $data['aspirations'] ?? '',
            'forDiscussion'     => $data['forDiscussion'] ?? '',
            'appraiserComments' => $data['appraiserComments'] ?? '',
        ]);
    }
}
