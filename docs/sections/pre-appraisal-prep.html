Section 16: Pre-Appraisal Preparation

The doctor prepares for the appraisal by summarising their evidence against the four GMC Good Medical Practice domains, plus a general comment field. This section is titled "Review of GMC Good Medical Practice domains" in the form navigation.

Section name: Section_16_preApprisalPrep
Fluent accessor: XfaPdf::preAppraisalPrep()
Navigation label: Review of GMC Good Medical Practice domains
Note the typo: The actual section name in the XFA template is Section_16_preApprisalPrep (with "Apprisal" instead of "Appraisal"). This typo exists in the original PDF form data and must be used exactly as-is when referencing the section programmatically. The fluent accessor preAppraisalPrep() handles this mapping for you.

What's in this section?

FieldTypeWhat it is
domain1TextareaSummary of knowledge, skills and performance
domain2TextareaSummary of safety and quality
domain3TextareaSummary of communication, partnership and teamwork
domain4TextareaSummary of maintaining trust
generalCommentTextareaGeneral comments and PDP draft ideas

This section has no repeatable items and no conditional fields. All five fields are standalone multiline text areas.

Setup

Before filling any fields, load the PDF in your controller:

use Xfa\Pdf\Facades\XfaPdf;

// Load the PDF file
XfaPdf::setFile(storage_path('app/xfa-pdf/appraisal.pdf'));

Reading existing data

Read all fields in this section at once:

// Read everything in Pre-Appraisal Preparation
$data = XfaPdf::read('Section_16_preApprisalPrep');

// Or using the fluent accessor
$data = XfaPdf::preAppraisalPrep()->read();

// $data looks like:
// [
//     'domain1' => 'I have maintained my clinical skills through...',
//     'domain2' => 'I have participated in clinical audit...',
//     'domain3' => 'I have worked collaboratively with colleagues...',
//     'domain4' => 'I have acted with honesty and integrity...',
//     'generalComment' => 'Key areas for next year\'s PDP include...',
// ]

Read a single field:

$domain1 = XfaPdf::preAppraisalPrep()->readField('domain1');
// 'I have maintained my clinical skills through...'

$general = XfaPdf::preAppraisalPrep()->readField('generalComment');
// 'Key areas for next year\'s PDP include...'

Domain Fields

The four domain fields correspond to the four domains of the GMC's Good Medical Practice framework. Each field is a multiline textarea where the doctor summarises their evidence and reflections for that domain.

Domain 1: Knowledge, skills and performance

Field: domain1

The doctor summarises their evidence relating to maintaining and developing their knowledge and skills. This includes CPD activities, clinical competence, and keeping up to date with current practice.

XfaPdf::preAppraisalPrep()->updateField(
    'domain1',
    'I have maintained my clinical skills through regular CPD activities '
    . 'including completion of the Advanced Life Support (ALS) course and '
    . 'the RCGP diabetes management update. I have attended 12 educational '
    . 'events this year and completed 52 hours of CPD. My clinical audit '
    . 'of antibiotic prescribing demonstrated that I am prescribing in '
    . 'line with NICE guidance. I have also completed the NHS Leadership '
    . 'Academy Edward Jenner programme to develop my leadership skills.'
);

Domain 2: Safety and quality

Field: domain2

The doctor summarises their evidence relating to patient safety and quality improvement. This includes audit activity, significant event analysis, and contributions to safe practice.

XfaPdf::preAppraisalPrep()->updateField(
    'domain2',
    'I have participated in clinical audit throughout the year. My '
    . 'antibiotic prescribing audit showed a 15% reduction in unnecessary '
    . 'prescriptions against the baseline. I contributed to the practice '
    . 'significant event review process and attended all monthly SEA '
    . 'meetings. I have been involved in two significant events this year, '
    . 'both of which were reviewed with appropriate learning identified '
    . 'and acted upon. I completed mandatory safeguarding training at '
    . 'Level 3 in October.'
);

Domain 3: Communication, partnership and teamwork

Field: domain3

The doctor summarises their evidence relating to communication with patients and colleagues, teamwork, and collaborative working relationships.

XfaPdf::preAppraisalPrep()->updateField(
    'domain3',
    'I have worked collaboratively with colleagues across the practice '
    . 'and the wider PCN. As Clinical Lead for the respiratory pathway, '
    . 'I have established regular MDT meetings with secondary care '
    . 'colleagues. Colleague feedback collected this year was positive, '
    . 'highlighting my approachability and willingness to support the '
    . 'team. Patient feedback via the Friends and Family Test showed '
    . '94% satisfaction. I have supervised two GP registrars this year '
    . 'with positive feedback from both.'
);

Domain 4: Maintaining trust

Field: domain4

The doctor summarises their evidence relating to probity, honesty, and maintaining patient and public trust. This includes declarations of interests, health status, and ethical conduct.

XfaPdf::preAppraisalPrep()->updateField(
    'domain4',
    'I have acted with honesty and integrity throughout the year. I '
    . 'have no complaints or concerns to declare. My probity statement '
    . 'is up to date and I have no conflicts of interest to declare '
    . 'beyond those already registered with the practice. I have '
    . 'maintained appropriate professional boundaries in all clinical '
    . 'interactions. My DBS check was renewed in September and remains '
    . 'clear. I confirm that my health does not affect my fitness to '
    . 'practise.'
);

General Comment

General comments and PDP draft ideas

Field: generalComment

A free-text area for any additional comments or draft ideas for the upcoming Personal Development Plan. The doctor can use this to flag topics for discussion at the appraisal meeting.

XfaPdf::preAppraisalPrep()->updateField(
    'generalComment',
    'Key areas for next year\'s PDP include:\n'
    . '- Completing the musculoskeletal diploma (currently 6 of 8 modules done)\n'
    . '- Developing skills in mental health assessment for primary care\n'
    . '- Expanding the PCN respiratory pathway to include COPD pulmonary rehab\n'
    . '- Undertaking a re-audit of antibiotic prescribing to measure sustained improvement\n\n'
    . 'I would also like to discuss the possibility of taking on a CCG advisory '
    . 'role for respiratory services from April next year.'
);

Complete Controller Example

Here's a full example filling the entire Pre-Appraisal Preparation section in a Laravel controller:

use Xfa\Pdf\Facades\XfaPdf;

class AppraisalController extends Controller
{
    public function fillPreAppraisalPrep(Request $request)
    {
        $pdfPath = storage_path('app/xfa-pdf/appraisal.pdf');
        XfaPdf::setFile($pdfPath);

        // -----------------------------------------------
        // 1. Fill all five domain and comment fields
        //    in a single update call
        // -----------------------------------------------
        XfaPdf::preAppraisalPrep()->update([
            'domain1' =>
                'I have maintained my clinical skills through regular '
                . 'CPD activities including completion of the ALS course '
                . 'and the RCGP diabetes management update. I attended '
                . '12 educational events and completed 52 hours of CPD. '
                . 'My antibiotic prescribing audit demonstrated that I '
                . 'am prescribing in line with NICE guidance.',

            'domain2' =>
                'I have participated in clinical audit throughout the '
                . 'year. My prescribing audit showed a 15% reduction in '
                . 'unnecessary prescriptions. I contributed to the '
                . 'practice significant event review process and attended '
                . 'all monthly SEA meetings. I completed mandatory '
                . 'safeguarding training at Level 3 in October.',

            'domain3' =>
                'I have worked collaboratively with colleagues across '
                . 'the practice and wider PCN. As Clinical Lead for the '
                . 'respiratory pathway, I established regular MDT '
                . 'meetings with secondary care. Colleague feedback was '
                . 'positive. Patient satisfaction via FFT was 94%. I '
                . 'supervised two GP registrars with positive feedback.',

            'domain4' =>
                'I have acted with honesty and integrity throughout the '
                . 'year. No complaints or concerns to declare. Probity '
                . 'statement is up to date with no conflicts of interest '
                . 'beyond those registered with the practice. DBS check '
                . 'renewed in September and remains clear. My health '
                . 'does not affect my fitness to practise.',

            'generalComment' =>
                'Key areas for next year\'s PDP: completing the MSK '
                . 'diploma, developing mental health assessment skills, '
                . 'expanding the PCN respiratory pathway to include COPD '
                . 'pulmonary rehab, and re-auditing antibiotic prescribing. '
                . 'I would also like to discuss taking on a CCG advisory '
                . 'role for respiratory services from April.',
        ]);

        // -----------------------------------------------
        // 2. Save the PDF
        // -----------------------------------------------
        XfaPdf::save();

        return redirect()->back()->with(
            'success',
            'Pre-Appraisal Preparation saved.'
        );
    }

    /**
     * Read and display existing Pre-Appraisal Preparation data.
     */
    public function showPreAppraisalPrep()
    {
        $pdfPath = storage_path('app/xfa-pdf/appraisal.pdf');
        XfaPdf::setFile($pdfPath);

        $data = XfaPdf::preAppraisalPrep()->read();

        return view('appraisal.pre-appraisal-prep', [
            'domain1'        => $data['domain1'] ?? '',
            'domain2'        => $data['domain2'] ?? '',
            'domain3'        => $data['domain3'] ?? '',
            'domain4'        => $data['domain4'] ?? '',
            'generalComment' => $data['generalComment'] ?? '',
        ]);
    }
}
